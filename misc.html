Akhil Bharat Vivekananda Yuva Mahamandal

At that time when I was a small kid, my home tutor Mr. Jagadish Dey used to go play ground everyday in the afternoon. He adviced me to accompany him to the ground. That play ground was not like a general play ground what usually people know. In that ground, Mr. Dey and few others used to teach us different moral and educational ideas through physical training, parried, drill, and games. Later on I came to know about the "Vivek Bahini", a children wings of Akhil Bharat Vivekananda Yuva Mahamandal, where I used to go with Mr. Dey. Henceforth, I was quick to get familiar with the life and teachings of Swami Vivekananda. Since then I am associated with the organization Akhil Bharat Vivekananda Yuva Mahamandal. Presently, I am serving as the secretary of Narayangarh Vivekananda Yuva Mahamandal, a branch of Akhil Bharat Vivekananda Yuva Mahamandal.
