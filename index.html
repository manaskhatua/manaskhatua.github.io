BIOGRAPHY::

Dr. Manas Khatua is working as Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur.

Prior to this, he was Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Singapore University of Technology and Design, Singapore. He also worked as Assistant System Engineer in TCS Kolkata and Bhubaneswar, India, and Lecturer in the Dept. of CSE, BUIE, Bankura, India.

He received his Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. His primary research interests are in the domain of Wireless Networks, Wireless Sensor Networks, Mobile Cloud Computing, Network Security, and Internet of Things. He is the author of many scholarly research papers, including reputed journal papers, in the above mentioned areas.
