Hello!

I am Manas.

Presently, I am pursuing postdoctoral research at Singapore University of Technology and Design. My primary research interests are in the domain of Wireless Network & Security, Wireless Sensor Network, and Mobile Cloud Computing.

I have received PhD from SIT, IIT Kharagpur, India, in September 2015.

Prior to that, I have worked as Assistant Systems Engineer of Banking & Financial Services in TCS, Kolkata and Bhubaneswar, India, and Lecturer in Dept. of CSE, BUIE, Bankura, India.

When I am not busy with these tasks, I often indulge in character building and philanthropic works organized by Akhil Bharat Vivekananda Yuva Mahamandal.

This website aims to create a personalized presence of myself on the Internet. Various aspects of my work and life would be highlighted here.
